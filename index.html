Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 Nov 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how TenSure (“we”, “us”) collects and uses information.

1. Data We Collect

We aim to collect as little personal data as possible. The App may store:

  • photos and documentation you upload,
  • app settings (e.g., language),
  • minimal device identifiers for analytics (if enabled),
  • saved reports stored locally on your device.

We do not sell personal data.
We do not track your location.
We do not require a user account unless explicitly added later.

2. Local Storage

Most user data is stored locally on your device and not automatically uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly choose to sync or backup (if such feature exists).

3. Optional Online Services (if applicable)

If cloud backup or sync services are added, we may process:

  • encrypted report data,
  • timestamps,
  • device metadata.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR-friendly)

If you are in the EU/EEA, processing is based on:

  • Legitimate interest (basic app functionality)
  • User consent (optional features like analytics)

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except:

  • service providers (e.g., cloud storage)
  • where required by law

These providers must comply with privacy rules.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA)

Depending on your region, you may have rights such as:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data
  • Download your data (portability)
  • Withdraw consent for analytics

8. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13.
